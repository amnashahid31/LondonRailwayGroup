Health and Safety Policy

LRG is committed to complying with the Health and Safety at Work etc Act 1974 and related laws to maintain a safe working environment.

KeyPoints:

• Manage health and safety across the business.
• Provide qualified resources for health and safety management.
• Equip employees with necessary training, equipment, and supervision.
• Set and monitor safety objectives through audits and performance reviews.
• Promote open discussions for continuous safety improvement.
• Aim to minimize occupational accidents and achieve a zero-accident workplace.
• Protect the health and safety of all visitors, contractors, and the public.
• Encourage employees to take responsibility for safety and report hazards.
• Foster continuous communication on safety at all levels.
• Support the reporting of accidents, incidents, and near misses.

The policy is reviewed annually and updated as needed.

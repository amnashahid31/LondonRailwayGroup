Anti-Discrimination Policy

LRG is committed to promoting an inclusive and respectful work environment free from all forms of discrimination.

KeyPoints:

• We strictly prohibit discrimination based on race, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, age, disability, national origin, or any other characteristic protected by law.
• We ensure equal opportunities in recruitment, promotion, training, and all aspects of employment.
• Any form of harassment or discriminatory behavior is not tolerated and will be addressed promptly.
• Employees are encouraged to report any discriminatory incidents without fear of retaliation.
• We provide regular training and resources to foster a culture of diversity and inclusion.
• Our policies align with relevant anti-discrimination laws to ensure compliance and promote fairness.

LRG is dedicated to maintaining a workplace where everyone feels valued, respected, and supported.

Employers

Staff Training


We provide training to relevant members of staff at Induction to ensure that they understand the risks of modern slavery infiltrating our business or supply chains and effective operation of our policies and procedures are aimed at mitigating this risk.

We make specific provision in our Whistleblowing Policy or the protection of those who whistleblow in relation to any issue.

Long Term Relations


We provide a trusted service based on long-term relationships.

LRG are able to provide top-quality temporary, contract and permanent staff to all types of organisations in a wide range of sectors. By building strong relationships with our clients, we get to know their specific recruitment challenges, and can offer a tailored service to help solve them.

Quality Processing


We ensure the completion of audits as part of the Quality process of our Company.We use the Risk Management processes to assess which of our own activities and supply chains represent the highest risks in respect of slavery or human trafficking, in particular, geography, sector and cost of supply.

Level of communication and personal contact with next link in the supply chain is maintained and understanding is established along with the compliance with our expectations.
